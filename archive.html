TIFFANY HUANG IS AN INTERDISCIPLINARY CREATOR THAT DABBLES IN THE VISUAL AND PERFORMING ARTS, DESIGN, CREATIVE CODING, AND WRITING. THROUGH HER WORK, SHE SEEKS TO TELL EVOCATIVE STORIES THAT RELATE TO THE SOCIETY SHE LIVES IN AND IS MOTIVATED PARTICULARLY BY MUNDANE BEAUTY, CULTURAL IDENTITY, AND THE HUMAN PSYCHE.

 

TIFFANY HUANG is an interdisciplinary creator that dabbles in the visual and performing arts, design, creative coding, and writing. through her work, she seeks to tell evocative stories that relate to the society she lives in and is motivated particularly by mundane beauty, cultural identity, and the human psyche.

Hello! My name is Tiffany Huang, and I'm an aspiring 21st-century Renaissance woman whose interests include psychology, computer science, creative writing, dance, music, design, fine arts, film and media, racial studies, and many more. When I manage to detach myself from my creative practice, I can usually be found binge-watching films and binge-reading novels in an effort to learn more about the world.
